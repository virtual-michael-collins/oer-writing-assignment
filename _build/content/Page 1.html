OER Writing Assignment

Description

This writing assignment asks students to critically reflect on the current issues mentioned in the course text.

Learning Objectives

  1. Gain better understanding of critical issues from course.
  2. Develop writing skills for critical discourse.

Instructions

  1. Re-read the course texts
  2. Choose two issues from the text that you would like to focus on
  3. Write a concise, 150 word reflection.
